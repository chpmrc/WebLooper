Note: this is a work in progress! Many expected features could not be working or be broken. Use at your own risk (yeah, it may blow up!)

Working features:

  • Volume: press the toggle corresponding to the track you want to change the volume of and scroll down/up. E.g. Q -> track 1, W -> track 2, etc.
  • Tempo detection: start recording the first track, the tempo will be automatically set depending after you stop the recording (currently 8 beats per measure/loop are supported)
